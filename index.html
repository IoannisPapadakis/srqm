Taught at Sciences Po in Paris by François Briatte and Ivaylo Petev.

What is this?

This page contains teaching material for the Statistical Reasoning and Quantitative Methods course. Stata and the course handbooks are not included due to copyright restrictions.

To install on your system, download the SRQM folder and expand it somewhere safe and accessible. Setup instructions are available from the README file of the folder.

Who is this for?

The SRQM folder, which we call the ‘Teaching Pack’ in class, is distributed to all students on their first week. It was made available as a GitHub repository for backup and sharing.

The repository wiki and the course blog provide additional links to inspirational material and learning resources. You are also invited to report issues with the code or course content if you detect any.

Contact

Please feel free to email your questions if you have any. Comments are also very welcome, especially from past students who now use Stata in their work, and there are a few!

Enjoy.
